Green Detox Smoothie

Description

This green smoothie is great for detoxing â€” it's super easy and very healthy. Don't be put off by the color, it actually tastes pretty good and is great for digestion.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/2 cup fresh spinach leaves
  • 3 broccoli florets
  • 1/4 pear, chopped
  • 1/4 green apple, chopped
  • 1/4 avocado, chopped

Steps

  1. Blend pineapple juice, spinach, broccoli florets, pear, apple, and avocado in a blender until smooth.
